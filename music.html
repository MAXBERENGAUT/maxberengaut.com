The Best 175 (Pop) Songs of the 2010s

By Max Berengaut
December 10, 2021

Over the course of the decade, (pop) music spread out across many genres, some new, some just reinterpretations of the old. The following works of art are the (pop) songs that have personally impacted me the most. As a result, the list and the ranking are biased, because they are based on my opinions. I have gaps in my music taste, just like everyone else. I also do not consider art to be at all related to the artist. Once it's created, it's no longer theirs, it's the world's and I think it's unfair to judge art by the character of the artist. I believe that people, especially nowadays, need to separate their personal opinions from the judgement of their art. This article is inspired of course by Pitchfork, but obviously, at its core, it's just another celebration of the art of music.
